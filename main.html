Live integration steps

  1. Create a Stripe account and two subscription products.
  2. Build a tiny backend to create Checkout Sessions.
  3. Listen for Stripe webhooks to confirm payment status.
  4. Store the subscription against the user account in your database.
  5. Only unlock premium views after your backend confirms the subscription.

Do not do this in the frontend

  • Do not place Stripe secret keys in `scripty.js`.
  • Do not trust local storage alone for paid access.
  • Do not expose AI API keys directly in the Electron app.
